What Is CladCamp?

CLADCAMP is a professional Craigslist Ad Design service that places a creative marketing team at the helm of your online craigslist advertising campaign. We can help you generate titles, write compelling content, develop brand awareness and launch a sucessful ad campaign. Ad agencies can charge thousands of dollars per month for these types of all inclusive services - our full service programs are as little as $20.00 per ad design.

Getting To Know You

In order to properly prpare your advertising campaign using CLADCAMP we need to understand your goals. We gather essential items including your company logo, your URL and any previous marketing that has been successful for your small business. It is important to us that your ads have the best chance at conversions - so we try to gather as much information as possible.

Designing Your Craigslist Ads

Each Craigslist ad design that we create starts with a blank canvas. We do not use templates or artwork / images from previous clients. You are always assured to get a 100% custom image ad that is unique to your small business. You can choose to have each ad be a stand alone ad or we can develop a series of ads that tell a story, similar to a powerpoint presentation.
